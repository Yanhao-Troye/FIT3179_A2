Worldwide Unicorn Companies

A unicorn means a privately owned startup valued at over $1 billion. The term "unicorn" has been widely used to refer to startups in technology, mobile technology, and information technology. As of 2024, there are already more than 1,200 unicorns in the world. Now, I'm going to show you some data about unicorns and hopefully provide you some insight into investments and business.

The distribution of the unicorn companies all over the world

From the proportional symbol map we find that USA have the highest number of unicorn companies which is 647, followed by China and India are the second and third countries that have the highest number of unicorn companies.

The countries with the top 5 unicorn companies and their industry flows

From the sankey chart we can observe that The US and China cover a wide range of industries, with most of the US unicorns being about internet softwarte and fintech, while China is more evenly split in every industry.

The most valuable companies and industries

Now, let's find out which companies and industries are most valuable. Having a higher value industry means that it may be a popular industry in today's society, but it also means that you will face higher competition if you want to join. From the lollipop chart, we can find that Byte dance and spacex both have high valuation. It shows that technology-related industries are relatively profitable industries today. And through bubble plot, we also proved that Internetsoftware, an industry related to the Internet, is indeed more valuable than other industries.

Investors of unicorn companies

Through this wordcloud, we can see which companies have invested in unicorns, and through the data we find that accel has invested in the most unicorns which is 64. Andreessen Horowitz followed which invest 59 unicorn companies.
